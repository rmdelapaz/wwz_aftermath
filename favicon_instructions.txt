# Favicon Placeholder

Replace `favicon.ico` in the root directory with your course icon.

## Creating a favicon:
1. Design a 16x16 or 32x32 pixel icon
2. Save as .ico, .png, or .svg format
3. Use online favicon generators for multiple sizes
4. Test in different browsers

## Recommended tools:
- favicon.io (online generator)
- RealFaviconGenerator.net
- GIMP or Photoshop for custom design
