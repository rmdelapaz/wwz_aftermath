ğŸŒŸ Tips for New Medic Players

  1. Stim early and often: Don't wait for critical health
  2. Learn stim range: It's longer than you think
  3. Watch everyone's health: Not just lowest player
  4. Position smartly: Middle of team, near cover
  5. Use Combat Medic: Free healing from kills
  6. Master Fighting Fit timing: Before big fights
  7. Don't forget your gun: You need to kill too
  8. Breathe Deeply changes everything: Area healing is powerful
  9. In the Zone is essential: 50% damage boost after healing
  10. Prestige priority: Remote Healthcare for range
