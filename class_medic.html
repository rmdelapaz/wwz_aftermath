Healer Support Team Survival

Core Role: Keep your team alive through heals, buffs, and clutch revives

üíâ Core Ability

Stim Pistol

Effect: Heal teammates from a distance with a temporary health and speed boost

Cooldown: 60 seconds (can be reduced with perks)

Pro Tip: Use proactively before fights, not just reactively when health is low!

üéØ Recommended Builds

üåü Beginner Build - "Stay Alive"

Focus on survivability and basic healing. Perfect for learning the class.

  • Priority Perks: Triage, Free Hugs, Sugar Coated, Fighting Fit
  • Playstyle: Stay near teammates, heal proactively, focus on survival
  • Weapons: Carbine + Compact SMG for versatility

üíö Pure Support Build - "Team Backbone"

Maximum healing output and team buffs. Ideal for coordinated teams.

  • Priority Perks: Paramedic, Efficiency, Pickpocket, Good Karma, Big Pharma
  • Playstyle: Manage resources, coordinate buffs, prioritize high-value targets
  • Weapons: Shotgun + Machine Pistol for close protection

‚öîÔ∏è Combat Medic Build - "Battle Angel"

Aggressive playstyle with self-sustain. For experienced players who can multitask.

  • Priority Perks: SMG Specialist, In the Zone, Combat Medic, Lobotomy
  • Playstyle: Chain kills for cooldown reset, aggressive positioning
  • Weapons: Dual SMGs for maximum fire rate

üßü Special Zombie Counter-Strategies

üêÇ Bull

  • Pre-heal teammates in Bull's path for damage reduction
  • Save Stim Pistol for instant recovery after charge
  • Position perpendicular to team, never in line
  • Stun grenades interrupt the slam animation

ü§¢ Gasbag

  • Maintain maximum distance - gas devastates teams
  • Pre-apply temp health before engaging
  • Have Stim ready for teammates caught in gas
  • Never melee - causes instant explosion

üëª Lurker

  • Listen for growls - audio cue before pounce
  • Travel in buddy system for immediate rescue
  • Masking gas makes you invisible during revives
  • Lobotomy perk allows Stim headshot kills

üò± Screamer

  • Priority target #1 - scream triggers swarm
  • Use Stim Pistol for silent ranged kills (with Lobotomy)
  • Always carry silenced weapon as Medic
  • If scream starts, prep AoE healing immediately

üìã Quick Reference

üíä Medkit Priority Order

  1. Pinned player - Immediate danger
  2. Last stand player - 5 second window
  3. Low health DPS - Maintains damage output
  4. Low health tank - Protects frontline
  5. Self (if under 50%) - Can't heal if dead

‚è±Ô∏è Stim Pistol Best Uses

  • Pre-swarm: Apply damage resistance buff
  • Mid-combat: Save grabbed teammates
  • Clutch revive: Instant health after pickup
  • Retreat: Speed boost for runners
  • Pyramid climber: Prevent team wipe

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Medic Mistakes to Avoid

  • Overhealing: Wasting medkits on 90% health teammates
  • Hoarding Stim: Not using it proactively (it's on cooldown!)
  • Bad positioning: Too far back to reach pinned teammates
  • Tunnel vision: Focusing only on healing, not killing
  • Poor economy: Using medkits when natural healing works

üåü Tips for New Medics

  1. Start with Normal difficulty - Learn healing timing without pressure
  2. Follow the tank - They know where danger comes from
  3. Communicate constantly - Call out your cooldowns
  4. Watch the kill feed - Shows who needs help
  5. Learn audio cues - Each special has unique sounds
  6. Practice Stim aim - It has travel time
  7. Don't chase kills - Your job is keeping everyone alive
