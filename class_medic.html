Swarm Defense Priorities:

  1. Set up near ammo box but not blocking it
  2. Pre-heal team for damage resistance
  3. Save medkits for mid-swarm emergencies
  4. Use Stim on pyramid climbers
  5. Focus fire on special zombies first

Running Section Priorities:

  1. Stay in middle of group - not front or back
  2. Heal while moving with Stim Pistol
  3. Drop medkits for slower teammates
  4. Save equipment for checkpoint defense
  5. Call out special spawns immediately

Boss Fight Priorities:

  1. Designate tank player to heal priority
  2. Manage medkit economy for phases
  3. Use Stim for burst damage phases
  4. Position for quick revives
  5. Save masking gas for critical revives

Escort Mission Priorities:

  1. One player pushes, Medic supports
  2. Rotate temp health on pusher
  3. Clear path with equipment
  4. Stim Pistol for emergency saves
  5. Maintain defensive perimeter

  • Overhealing: Wasting medkits on 90% health teammates
  • Hoarding Stim: Not using it proactively (it's on cooldown, use it!)
  • Bad positioning: Too far back to reach pinned teammates

üåü Tips for New Medics

  1. Start with Normal difficulty - Learn healing timing without pressure
  2. Follow the tank - They know where danger comes from
  3. Communicate constantly - Call out your cooldowns
  4. Watch the kill feed - Tells you who needs help
  5. Learn audio cues - Each special has unique sounds
  6. Practice aim with Stim - It has travel time
  7. Don't chase kills - Your job is keeping everyone up
