üìä Virus Sample System Overview

Virus samples are account-wide collectibles that permanently unlock powerful upgrades in the Virus Sample Lab.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Difficulty Requirement

Virus samples ONLY spawn on Hard difficulty or higher!

  • Not available on Easy or Normal difficulty
  • Available on Hard, Insane, and Extreme
  • Same locations regardless of difficulty (Hard+)
  • Once collected, permanently unlocked for your account
  • Can be collected in any order

üèÜ Virus Sample Rewards

Each virus sample you collect can be spent in the Virus Sample Lab to unlock permanent upgrades:

Available Upgrades (1 sample each):

  • Health Boost I-V: +5% health per level (max +25%)
  • Equipment Cooldown I-V: -3% cooldown per level (max -15%)
  • Melee Damage I-V: +5% damage per level (max +25%)
  • Reload Speed I-V: +3% speed per level (max +15%)
  • Weapon Damage I-V: +2% damage per level (max +10%)
  • Special Infected Resistance I-V: +10% resistance per level (max +50%)

Note: These upgrades apply to ALL classes and persist across all game modes!

üí° Virus Sample Hunting Tips

  • Difficulty: Must play on Hard or higher - samples don't spawn on Easy/Normal
  • Visual Cues: Look for glowing green vials, often in hard-to-reach places
  • Audio Cues: Samples emit a faint bubbling sound when nearby
  • Team Collection: Only one player needs to collect it for the whole team
  • Exploration: Most samples are off the main path - explore thoroughly
  • Before Defense: Many are placed before triggering defense sequences
  • Class Choice: Use Slasher for speed or Medic for survivability
  • Private Lobbies: Use private games to explore without pressure
  • Checkpoint System: Samples save at checkpoints - you don't need to finish the mission

üìù Important Notes
