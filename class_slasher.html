Identity & role

Role

Melee control and stuns to protect the line.

Why pick

Low ammo stress; great when learning positioning and crowd control.

At a glance

Starter build (new player friendly)

Designed to be safe and simple. Exact perk names vary by patch; focus on the effect described.

  • Choose perks that buff melee damage/stagger and reduce incoming damage while swinging.
  • Carry a shotgun or SMG for specials that you shouldn’t melee.
  • Grab stuns that reset the line when a bull/charger appears.

Pair with teammates who can cover your weaknesses (see Classes Hub for synergies).

Simple gameplay loop

flowchart LR Scan[Scan the lane] --> Prep[Pre-place utility] Prep --> Engage[Engage the horde] Engage --> Stabilize[Stabilize with class tools] Stabilize --> Recover[Ammo/Heal/Regroup] Recover --> Scan

Tips

  • Don’t overextend—hold just ahead of the team to catch stragglers.
  • Time swings to interrupt lunges; back up during reloads.
  • Ping bulls; step aside for teammates to shoot weak points.
