üåü Tips for New Slasher Players

  1. Melee never slows: Your unique advantage - keep swinging!
  2. Double damage when fresh: First strikes hit hardest
  3. Stun Gun timing: Stun groups BEFORE engaging
  4. Rooted is essential: Regular zombies can't pin you
  5. Chain kills matter: 10-kill chains trigger multiple bonuses
  6. Low health power: You get stronger when hurt
  7. Team melee boost: Level 10 helps everyone
  8. Shock Therapy combo: Stun + melee = no fatigue
  9. Silent Killer: Start with silenced SMG for stealth
  10. Prestige priority: Executioner III for extra strikes
