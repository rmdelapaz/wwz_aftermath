üó°Ô∏è Slasher Skill Tree

How the Skill System Works:
  • Progress through levels 1-30 to unlock new perk segments
  • Choose ONE perk per segment to activate
  • Red perks are special alternatives that cost more (red coins)
  • You can buy multiple perks in each segment but only use one at a time
  • Perks cost 150-500 yellow coins, red perks cost 150-500 red coins
  • Level 10, 20, and 30 perks are automatic and always active

‚≠ê Prestige Rank Perks

About Prestige Perks:
  • Unlocked by reaching Prestige Ranks 1-9
  • These are PASSIVE bonuses that are always active once unlocked
  • Stack with your regular perks for even more power
  • Each costs 7500 yellow coins to unlock
  • Persist through prestige resets

Prestige Progression

Rank 1: Juiced Up II
Stun Gun effect duration increased by 15%.
Stacks with regular Juiced Up for massive stun duration.
Rank 2: Zeus II
Increase Stun Gun range by 15% (base: 12 meters).
Combined with Zeus, your stun gun becomes a room-clearer.
Rank 3: Executioner III
Perform 1 more melee strike before becoming tired.
More swings = more damage before needing to rest.
Rank 4: Nimble II
Take 10% less damage while performing melee attacks.
Stacks with Nimble for 60% damage reduction during melee!
Rank 6: Flash Sale II
Killing a stunned special zombie restores 5% of your primary weapon's ammunition.
Combines stun gun usage with ammo restoration.
Rank 7: Get Lost!
Electric Grids can kill 5% more zombies for the whole team.
Team-wide benefit for defense sections.
Rank 8: Swapping Mags II
SMG reload speed is increased by 5%.
Small but helpful boost to your ranged backup.
Rank 9: Wheatgrass II
Health increased by 5%.
Stacks with regular Wheatgrass for 30% more health total.
üí° Prestige Strategy: Focus on reaching at least Prestige Rank 4 for Nimble II - the damage reduction while meleeing is crucial for higher difficulties. Executioner III at Rank 3 is also excellent for sustained melee combat. The later ranks offer nice bonuses but are less essential.

üìç Situational Tactics

üåä Swarm Defense

  1. Hold choke points with Rooted perk
  2. Use doorways to limit attack angles
  3. Save Stun Gun for pyramid climbers
  4. Shock Therapy for infinite melee during swarm
  5. Slice and Dice helps entire team melee longer

üèÉ Running Sections

  1. All Elbows prevents slowdown
  2. Lead with melee to clear path
  3. Stun Gun backward for chasers
  4. Clear! can save pinned teammates instantly
  5. Never stop moving forward

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Slasher Mistakes to Avoid

  • Melee spam: Time your swings, don't button mash
  • Fighting Gasbags in melee: Instant death for you and team!
  • Wasting Stun Gun: Save for emergencies and specials
  • Ignoring fatigue: Pace yourself, use Shock Therapy combo
  • Poor positioning: Use walls and doorways to limit angles
  • Not using ranged backup: SMG for distant threats

üåü Tips for New Slasher Players

  1. Master positioning - Doorways make you invincible
  2. Learn melee timing - Wait for groups, don't spam
  3. Manage stamina - Shock Therapy combo is key
  4. Save Stun Gun - It's your panic button
  5. Use Rooted wisely - Stand your ground when needed
  6. Communicate pins - Team needs to know you're stuck
  7. Practice heavy attacks - They stagger specials
  8. Stay with team - You're the wall, not a solo hero
