üéØ Recommended Builds

üåü Beginner Build - "Survivor"

Focus on staying alive while learning melee mechanics.

  • Priority: Health and crowd control
  • Key Focus: Rooted, All Elbows, Vigorous, Bullet Dodger
  • Playstyle: Tank damage, control crowds, support team
  • Weapons: Machete + Shotgun for backup
üí° Tip: You can tank entire swarms with proper positioning. Stand in doorways!

‚ö° Zeus Build - "Electric God"

Maximum stun gun effectiveness. Control entire areas with electricity.

  • Priority: Stun gun range, damage, and duration
  • Key Focus: Zeus, High Voltage, Juiced Up, Disarm
  • Playstyle: Save stun for emergencies and special kills
  • Weapons: Any melee + SMG for range
üí° Tip: Fully upgraded stun gun can clear 20 zombies instantly!

üó°Ô∏è Samurai Build - "Blade Master"

Pure melee focus with health sustain. Never stop swinging.

  • Priority: Melee damage, speed, and healing
  • Key Focus: Samurai, Heavy Strike, Whirlwind, Trophy Hunter
  • Playstyle: Constant melee combat, heal through damage
  • Weapons: Katana or Chainsaw when available
üí° Tip: Samurai perk gives temp health per kill - you become immortal in swarms!

üëª Assassin Build - "Silent Blade"

Stealth melee with hit-and-run tactics.

  • Priority: Invisibility and speed
  • Key Focus: Cloak and Dagger, Nimble, Bloodlust
  • Playstyle: Silent kills, reposition, repeat
  • Weapons: Knife + Silenced weapons
üí° Tip: Chain silent kills to stay invisible almost permanently!

üßü Special Zombie Counter-Strategies

üêÇ Bull

  • Stun Gun instantly stops charge
  • Sidestep and melee the back
  • Can tank a charge with enough health
  • Flash grenades blind for easy kill

ü§¢ Gasbag

  • NEVER melee - instant gas explosion!
  • Use ranged weapon or stun gun
  • Let teammates handle from distance
  • If forced close, stun and run

üëª Lurker

  • All Elbows perk prevents easy pounce
  • Quick melee breaks grapple
  • Stun Gun reveals and kills
  • Listen for growl, spin and melee

üò± Screamer

  • Sprint and melee before scream
  • Stun Gun for instant silent kill
  • Throw stun grenade if too far
  • Cloak and Dagger for stealth approach

üßü Infector

  • Priority melee target - stops spit
  • Dodge sideways, not backwards
  • Stun to interrupt spit animation
  • One heavy melee usually kills

üìç Situational Tactics

üåä Swarm Defense

  1. Hold the frontline at choke points
  2. Save stun gun for pyramid climbers
  3. Use doorways to limit attack angles
  4. Let team shoot over your shoulder
  5. Rotate to rest stamina between waves

üèÉ Running Sections

  1. Lead the charge - you're the tank
  2. Clear path with melee swings
  3. Stun gun backwards at chasers
  4. All Elbows means no slowdown
  5. Drop stun grenades at corners

üëπ Boss Fights

  1. Handle adds while team focuses boss
  2. Stun gun for emergency add clear
  3. Tank boss melee attacks if needed
  4. Create space for teammates
  5. Samurai perk sustains through damage

üõ°Ô∏è Escort Missions

  1. Clear melee range around objective
  2. You push while team covers
  3. Stun gun for emergency stops
  4. Tank zombies off the objective
  5. Rooted perk prevents interruption

üìã Quick Reference

üó°Ô∏è Melee Combat Tips

  • Swing timing: Wait for zombies to group
  • Stamina management: Don't spam, time swings
  • Positioning: Back to wall, face the horde
  • Target priority: Specials first, then crowds
  • Combo attacks: Heavy + light + heavy

‚ö° Stun Gun Best Uses

  • Pyramid climbers: Instant team save
  • Pinned teammate: Instant rescue
  • Bull charge: Instant stop
  • Swarm breach: Emergency clear
  • Special killer: With Disarm perk

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Slasher Mistakes to Avoid

  • Melee spam: Wastes stamina, time your swings
  • Fighting Gasbags: Never melee them!
  • Poor positioning: Getting surrounded with no escape
  • Ignoring stamina: Always keep some reserve
  • Wasting stun gun: Save for emergencies
  • Tunnel vision: Watch for specials while slashing
  • Solo warrior: Stay with team, you're not invincible

üåü Tips for New Slasher Players

  1. Master positioning - Doorways and corners are your friend
  2. Learn melee timing - Don't button mash
  3. Manage stamina - Always keep some for emergencies
  4. Prioritize specials - Your job is to protect the team
  5. Use the stun gun wisely - It's your panic button
  6. Stay with your team - You tank, they shoot
  7. Learn enemy sounds - React before you see them
  8. Practice melee combos - Heavy attacks stagger more
