üéØ Recommended Builds

üåü Beginner Build - "Spray and Pray"

Focus on ammo efficiency and forgiveness. Perfect for learning positioning.

  • Priority: Ammo regeneration and reload speed
  • Key Focus: Power Nap, Swapping Mags, movement perks
  • Playstyle: Constant weapon swapping, mobile shooting
  • Weapons: Assault Rifle + SMG for versatility
üí° Tip: Let Power Nap reload your weapons while you use the other. Never stop shooting!

üéØ Headshot Build - "Precision Marksman"

Maximum efficiency through headshots. For players with good aim.

  • Priority: Headshot bonuses and ammo refunds
  • Key Focus: Front Line Supply, Eagle Eye, Thrifty
  • Playstyle: Careful shots, infinite ammo through headshots
  • Weapons: Battle Rifle + Pistol for precision
üí° Tip: With proper headshot chains, you literally never need to reload!

üí• Grenade Master Build - "Boom Boom"

Focus on explosive crowd control. Great for tight maps and swarms.

  • Priority: Grenade enhancements and equipment
  • Key Focus: Fire In The Hole, Impact Grenades, Happy Hour
  • Playstyle: Save grenades for pyramids and special spawns
  • Weapons: SMG + Grenade Launcher when available
üí° Tip: Impact grenades can instantly clear pyramid bases!

‚ö° Speed Demon Build - "Run and Gun"

Maximum mobility while maintaining damage. For aggressive players.

  • Priority: Movement speed and weapon handling
  • Key Focus: Slippery, Nimble, Seek and Destroy
  • Playstyle: Constant movement, never stop to aim
  • Weapons: Dual SMGs for maximum mobility
üí° Tip: You can outrun most zombies while shooting backwards!

üßü Special Zombie Counter-Strategies

üêÇ Bull

  • Constant headshots during charge wind-up for quick kill
  • Use Slippery perk to sidestep while shooting
  • Save grenades for emergency Bull stops
  • Focus fire the head once visor breaks

ü§¢ Gasbag

  • Maximum range engagement always
  • Headshot chains prevent approach
  • Never use grenades - causes instant explosion
  • Welcoming Committee perk melts them instantly

üëª Lurker

  • Listen for audio cues while shooting
  • Quick 180 spins to check behind
  • Grenades flush them from corners
  • Seek and Destroy speed helps escape

üò± Screamer

  • PRIORITY TARGET - instant headshot required
  • Always carry silenced secondary
  • Eagle Eye perk helps quick acquisition
  • If scream starts, grenade the swarm spawn

üéØ Bomber

  • NEVER shoot the body - headshots only
  • Let them get close then sidestep
  • Leg shots to stumble if needed
  • Grenades at feet don't trigger explosion

üìç Situational Tactics

üåä Swarm Defense

  1. Find elevated position with good sightlines
  2. Pre-damage approaching waves at range
  3. Save grenades for pyramid bases
  4. Use Power Nap between waves
  5. Focus specials first, always

üèÉ Running Sections

  1. Sprint-shoot constantly (no accuracy penalty)
  2. Clear path ahead for team
  3. Throw grenades backwards at chasers
  4. Swap weapons instead of reloading
  5. Call out specials immediately

üëπ Boss Fights

  1. Maintain constant DPS on weak points
  2. Clear adds quickly between phases
  3. Save grenades for add waves
  4. Use high-damage weapons for boss phases
  5. Swap weapons to avoid reload downtime

üõ°Ô∏è Escort Missions

  1. Clear path ahead constantly
  2. Circle-strafe around objective
  3. Grenades for emergency clears
  4. Headshot chains to conserve ammo
  5. Never stop moving or shooting

üìã Quick Reference

üéØ Target Priority

  1. Screamer - Always first, prevent swarms
  2. Gasbag - Range threat to team
  3. Bull - Can wipe team if ignored
  4. Lurker - Disrupts your DPS
  5. Regular zombies - Constant thinning

‚ö° Movement Tips

  • Circle-strafe: Move in circles while shooting center
  • Backpedal: Shoot while retreating
  • Jump-shot: Jump over zombies while shooting down
  • Slide-shoot: Maintain accuracy while sliding
  • Corner-peek: Use corners to funnel zombies

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Gunslinger Mistakes to Avoid

  • Standing still: You have no accuracy penalty while moving!
  • Reloading too much: Swap weapons instead
  • Ignoring headshots: They refund ammo!
  • Wasting grenades: Save for pyramids and emergencies
  • Poor positioning: Always need escape routes
  • Tunnel vision: Check flanks constantly
  • Not using Power Nap: Free reloads while using other gun

üåü Tips for New Gunslinger Players

  1. Always be moving - Standing still = death
  2. Aim for the head - Headshots refund ammo and deal more damage
  3. Master weapon swapping - Faster than reloading
  4. Learn spawn points - Pre-aim where zombies appear
  5. Manage grenades - Don't waste on small groups
  6. Position smartly - High ground and escape routes
  7. Communicate specials - You see them first at range
  8. Practice circle-strafing - Key survival technique
