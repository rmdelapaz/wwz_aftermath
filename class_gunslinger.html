üéØ Gunslinger Skill Tree

How the Skill System Works:
  • Progress through levels 1-30 to unlock new perk segments
  • Choose ONE perk per segment to activate
  • Red perks are special alternatives that cost more (red coins)
  • You can buy multiple perks in each segment but only use one at a time
  • Perks cost 150-500 yellow coins, red perks cost 150-500 red coins
  • Level 10, 20, and 30 perks are automatic and always active

‚≠ê Prestige Rank Perks

About Prestige Perks:
  • Unlocked by reaching Prestige Ranks 1-9
  • These are PASSIVE bonuses that are always active once unlocked
  • Stack with your regular perks for even more power
  • Each costs 7500 yellow coins to unlock
  • Persist through prestige resets

Prestige Progression

Rank 1: Swapping Mags II
Reload speed increased by 10% on primary weapons.
Stacks with regular Swapping Mags for 35% total reload speed.
Rank 2: Swapping Mags III
Reload speed increased by 15% on secondary weapons.
Makes weapon swapping even more viable for sustained DPS.
Rank 3: Deep Pockets II
Carried ammo capacity for primary and secondary weapons increased by 10%.
Stacks with Deep Pockets for 35% more ammo capacity.
Rank 4: Fire in The Hole II
Frag Grenade can kill 20% more targets (base: 14).
Combined with Fire in the Hole, grenades become crowd-clearing monsters.
Rank 6: My Bad II
Take 10% less friendly fire damage.
Reduces incoming friendly fire - great for chaotic teams.
Rank 7: I Won't Budge From This Spot!
Fully depleting a Stationary Machine Gun has 10% chance to receive a new one (5% in Horde mode).
Potentially infinite stationary gun usage for defense sections.
Rank 8: Hold the Line!
The recoil of primary and secondary weapons is reduced by 10%.
Better accuracy for sustained fire, especially with automatic weapons.
Rank 9: Sleight of Hand
Switch weapons 25% faster (stacks with regular Sleight of Hand but capped at 150%).
The ultimate weapon-swapping speed for Power Nap builds.
üí° Prestige Strategy: Focus on reaching at least Prestige Rank 4 for the core reload and ammo improvements. Fire in The Hole II at Rank 4 is particularly valuable for crowd control. The later ranks offer nice quality-of-life improvements but are less essential.

üìã Key Perk Details

Must-Have Perks

Power Nap (Level 1-3)
Unequipped weapon automatically reloads after 7 seconds.
One of the best Gunslinger perks - essentially infinite shooting by weapon swapping.
Front Line Supply (Level 4-6)
Headshots made with primary or secondary weapons automatically restore 5% rounds to the magazine.
With good aim, you literally never need to reload.
Switcheroo (Level 27-29)
Switching to primary or secondary weapons increases firearm damage by 50% for 5 seconds.
Combines perfectly with Power Nap for massive damage while never reloading.
Bounty Hunter (Level 11-13)
Deal 25% more damage to special zombies.
Essential for higher difficulties where specials are the main threat.
Sort It All Out (Level 11-13 - Red)
Emptying the magazine of the primary weapon loads the secondary weapon with explosive rounds.
Creates a powerful combo - empty primary, switch to explosive secondary.

üéØ Recommended Builds

üåü Beginner Build - "Never Stop Shooting"

Focus on ammo efficiency and continuous fire. Perfect for learning the class.

Essential Perks:
  • Level 1-3: Power Nap (Auto-reload unequipped weapons)
  • Level 4-6: Swapping Mags! (25% faster reload)
  • Level 11-13: Sleight of Hand (150% faster weapon swap)
  • Level 21-23: Deep Pockets (25% more ammo)
  • Level 27-29: Annihilator (15% more damage)
üí° Tip: Constantly swap between weapons - one shoots while the other reloads automatically!
Best Prestige Perks: Swapping Mags II & III (Ranks 1-2), Deep Pockets II (Rank 3).

üéØ Headshot Build - "Precision Marksman"

Maximum efficiency through headshots. For players with good aim.

Essential Perks:
  • Level 4-6: Front Line Supply (Headshots restore ammo)
  • Level 11-13: Bounty Hunter (25% more damage to specials)
  • Level 14-16: Concentration (5 headshots grant temp health)
  • Level 24-26: Headhunter Reward (5 headshots refill equipment)
  • Level 27-29: Switcheroo (50% damage on weapon swap)
üí° Tip: Chain headshots for infinite ammo, health, and equipment!
Best Prestige Perks: Hold the Line! (Rank 8) for recoil reduction, helps with headshots.

üí• Explosive Build - "Grenade Specialist"

Maximum grenade effectiveness and regeneration.

Essential Perks:
  • Level 1-3: Fire in the Hole (25% more grenade kills)
  • Level 11-13 Red: Sort It All Out (Explosive rounds on secondary)
  • Level 24-26: Work Belt (3 grenades max)
  • Level 24-26 Alt: Pocket Factory (Equipment regenerates)
  • Level 21-23: Free Refill (Equipment kills restore ammo)
üí° Tip: Save grenades for pyramids and dense crowds. Use regeneration to always have equipment ready.
Best Prestige Perks: Fire in The Hole II (Rank 4) for even more grenade kills.

‚ö° Last Stand Build - "Action Hero"

Become unstoppable when you're the last one standing.

Essential Perks:
  • Level 14-16 Red: Encore (75% temp health instead of incapacitation)
  • Level 21-23: Action Hero (Infinite ammo when last standing)
  • Level 24-26 Red: It's Not Over (Infinite equipment when last standing)
  • Level 11-13: Wheatgrass (25% more health)
  • Level 27-29: Annihilator (15% more damage)
üí° Tip: When teammates fall, you become a one-person army with infinite resources!
Best Prestige Perks: My Bad II (Rank 6) to survive friendly fire when reviving.

üìç Situational Tactics

üåä Swarm Defense

  1. Find elevated position with good sightlines
  2. Use Power Nap - swap weapons instead of reloading
  3. Save grenades for pyramid bases
  4. Switcheroo damage bonus at swarm start
  5. Focus specials first with Bounty Hunter

üèÉ Running Sections

  1. Sprint-shoot constantly (no accuracy penalty)
  2. Front Line Supply for infinite ammo while moving
  3. Impact grenades thrown backward at chasers
  4. Swap weapons instead of reloading
  5. Seek and Destroy marks threats automatically

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Gunslinger Mistakes to Avoid

  • Standing still: You have no accuracy penalty while moving!
  • Reloading constantly: Use Power Nap and weapon swapping
  • Ignoring headshots: Front Line Supply gives infinite ammo
  • Wasting grenades: Save for pyramids and dense crowds
  • Not using Switcheroo: 50% damage boost is huge
  • Poor positioning: Stay mobile with escape routes

üåü Tips for New Gunslinger Players

  1. Always be moving - You shoot perfectly while sprinting
  2. Master weapon swapping - Power Nap means never reloading
  3. Aim for the head - Front Line Supply = infinite ammo
  4. Learn grenade physics - Impact vs regular grenades
  5. Use Seek and Destroy - Auto-marking is invaluable
  6. Save equipment for crowds - Don't waste on singles
  7. Position for sightlines - You're the team's ranged DPS
  8. Call out specials - You see them first with marking
