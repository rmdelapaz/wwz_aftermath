üéÆ General Questions

What's the difference between World War Z and World War Z: Aftermath?

Aftermath is a major expansion/upgrade to the original World War Z that includes:

  • All original content plus new campaigns (Rome, Kamchatka)
  • First-person mode option (can toggle between third and first person)
  • Two new classes: Vanguard (shield tank) and Dronemaster (drone support)
  • Melee weapon system with dual-wielding options
  • Horde Mode XL - endless survival mode
  • New special zombie: Rats (environmental hazard in new maps)
  • 4K and 60 FPS on next-gen consoles

If you own the original WWZ, you can upgrade to Aftermath at a discounted price.

Is World War Z: Aftermath worth it in 2024/2025?

Yes, especially if you enjoy:

  • Left 4 Dead style co-op gameplay
  • Massive zombie hordes (500+ on screen)
  • Class-based team gameplay
  • Regular content updates and seasonal events

The game has an active player base, regular updates, and full crossplay support. It frequently goes on sale for $15-20, which is excellent value for the content.

Can I play solo/offline?

Yes! The game supports:

  • Offline mode with AI teammates (requires initial online activation)
  • Private lobbies with AI filling empty slots
  • AI teammates that are surprisingly competent
Note: Challenge Mode and weekly challenges require online connection. Progression is slower offline as you miss out on multiplayer bonuses.
How long is the campaign?

The complete Aftermath campaign includes:

  • 7 Episodes with 3-4 missions each
  • Total: About 25-30 missions
  • Playtime: 15-20 hours for first playthrough
  • 100% Completion: 50+ hours (all difficulties, challenges)

Each mission takes 20-40 minutes depending on difficulty and team skill.

‚öîÔ∏è Gameplay & Combat Questions

What difficulty should I start on?

Recommended progression:

  • First time: Normal (learn mechanics without frustration)
  • After 2-3 missions: Hard (better rewards, more engaging)
  • Level 10+ with perks: Insane (significant challenge jump)
  • Prestige 4+ coordinated team: Extreme (requires mastery)
üí° Pro Tip: Hard difficulty is the sweet spot for fun and rewards once you understand basics. Normal becomes too easy very quickly.
How does friendly fire work?

Friendly fire scales with difficulty:

  • Easy: 0% (no friendly fire)
  • Normal: 0%
  • Hard: 10% damage to teammates
  • Insane: 25% damage
  • Extreme: 50% damage
‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Explosives and heavy weapons always deal full friendly fire damage regardless of percentage!
What are pyramids and how do I stop them?

Pyramids are zombie climbing structures that form during swarms:

  • Zombies stack on each other to reach high places
  • Form at walls, fences, and elevated positions

How to stop them:

  • Shoot the base - Collapsing bottom kills entire pyramid
  • Explosives - One grenade/C4 clears whole pyramid
  • Mortars/MGs - Positioned defenses auto-target pyramids
  • Electric grids - Prevent climbing at that spot
Why did I get grabbed through a wall/fence?

This is a game mechanic, not a bug:

  • Zombies can grab through thin barriers (fences, windows)
  • Maintain at least 1 meter distance from barriers
  • Some walls have "safe zones" - learn them per map
  • Lurkers can pounce through some obstacles
Tip: Never stand directly against fences during swarms. Always leave grab distance.

üé≠ Classes & Builds Questions

What's the best class for beginners?

Top 3 beginner-friendly classes:

  1. Medic: Keep yourself and team alive, always useful
  2. Gunslinger: Simple gameplay - just shoot accurately
  3. Exterminator: Fire control is intuitive and effective

Avoid initially:

  • Slasher: Requires good positioning and game knowledge
  • Hellraiser: Easy to damage team with explosives
Can I reset my class perks?

No, but:

  • You can buy ALL perks eventually (no permanent lock-out)
  • Yellow coins are farmable - just takes time
  • Prestiging resets perks but gives permanent red perks
  • You can switch active perks anytime between missions
Tip: Don't stress about "wrong" choices. You'll eventually unlock everything.
Should I prestige my class?

Yes, but timing matters:

  • Prestige 1-4: Essential for red perks (permanent bonuses)
  • Prestige 5: Unlocks unique class weapon skin
  • Prestige 6-9: New red perks added in 2024 update

Best strategy:

  1. Save 30,000+ yellow coins
  2. Prestige to level 4 immediately
  3. Buy crucial perks back
  4. Continue to Prestige 5+ later
Warning: You lose ALL purchased perks when prestiging! Save coins first!
What's the best class composition for a team?

Ideal 4-player team composition:

  • 1 Medic: Essential for higher difficulties
  • 1 Fixer: Ammo and equipment support
  • 2 DPS/Control: Mix of Hellraiser, Exterminator, Gunslinger

Alternative strong compositions:

  • Tank comp: Vanguard + Slasher + Medic + Fixer
  • Explosive comp: 2 Hellraisers + Fixer + Medic
  • Balanced: One from each category (support/tank/DPS/control)

üìà Progression & Unlocks Questions

What's the fastest way to level up?

XP optimization tips:

  • Hard/Insane difficulty: Best XP-to-time ratio
  • Full team bonus: +10% XP with 4 players
  • No damage taken: Bonus XP for health remaining
  • Headshot kills: Extra XP per headshot
  • Special zombie kills: Worth 5-10x normal zombies

Best farming mission: "Dead Sea Stroll" (Jerusalem 2) - short with guaranteed swarms

How do I get blue/yellow coins faster?

Blue Coins (Supplies):

  • Complete missions (scales with difficulty)
  • Daily/Weekly challenges
  • Horde Mode waves
  • First-time map completion bonuses

Yellow Coins (Class Points):

  • Level up any class (1000 per level)
  • Complete challenge mode missions
  • Weekly challenge rewards
  • Can use on ANY class (not locked)
Tip: Level multiple classes to 30 for maximum yellow coin generation.
What should I spend credits on first?

Priority order:

  1. Class perks - Biggest gameplay impact
  2. Favorite weapon upgrades - Tier 3 is sufficient initially
  3. Virus samples - For weapon variants
  4. Cosmetics - Only after gameplay upgrades
Don't: Spread upgrades thin. Focus on one class and 2-3 weapons initially.

üë• Multiplayer & Co-op Questions

Does the game have crossplay?

Yes! Full crossplay between:

  • PC (Steam/Epic Games Store)
  • PlayStation 4/5
  • Xbox One/Series X|S
  • Nintendo Switch (limited to 30 FPS)

How to enable: Settings ‚Üí Online ‚Üí Crossplay (ON by default)

Note: PC players can use text chat, console players need voice chat or preset commands.
How do I play with friends?

Multiple options:

  • Invite Code: Host creates code, friends enter it
  • Friends List: Direct invite through platform
  • Private Lobby: Set to "Friends Only" or "Invite Only"

Cross-platform play: Use the in-game friend code system, not platform invites.

Why can't I find any players?

Common solutions:

  • Enable crossplay - Massively increases player pool
  • Change region - Try US East or Europe
  • Play popular missions - Not obscure challenge modes
  • Prime time - Evenings and weekends are busiest
  • Lower difficulty - More players on Hard than Extreme
Quick Match is fastest for finding games. Choose class after joining.

üîß Technical & Performance Questions

What are the system requirements?

PC Minimum:

  • CPU: Core i3-3220 / AMD A10-5700
  • GPU: GeForce GTX 650 Ti / Radeon HD 5870
  • RAM: 8 GB
  • Storage: 50 GB

PC Recommended:

  • CPU: Core i7-3970 / AMD FX-9370
  • GPU: GeForce GTX 1060 / Radeon R9 380
  • RAM: 16 GB

Console Performance:

  • PS5/XSX: 4K 60 FPS or 1080p 120 FPS
  • PS4/XB1: 1080p 30 FPS
  • Switch: 720p 30 FPS (docked)
How do I toggle first-person mode?

Controls:

  • PC: Press V
  • Console: Click right stick (R3)

Settings: Gameplay ‚Üí Camera ‚Üí Default View Mode

Note: You can switch anytime during gameplay. Some prefer third-person for awareness, first-person for aiming.
Why is my game lagging/stuttering?

Common fixes:

  • Lower Zombie Count: Settings ‚Üí Video ‚Üí Zombie Count
  • Disable Vsync: Can cause input lag
  • Vulkan Mode: Often better performance than DX11
  • Close background apps: Especially browsers
  • Verify game files: Through Steam/Epic
Known issue: Memory leak after multiple missions. Restart game every 2-3 hours.

üèÜ Endgame & Challenge Questions

What is Horde Mode XL?

Endless survival mode with:

  • Progressively harder waves
  • Shop system between waves
  • Leaderboards for competition
  • Unique rewards and achievements

Tips for success:

  • Save credits for later waves
  • Buy defenses > weapons early
  • Coordinate class abilities
  • Wave 25+ requires perfect teamwork
What are Mutators and Challenge Mode?

Challenge Mode: Weekly rotating missions with special modifiers

Common Mutators:

  • Six Skulls: All zombies are special infected
  • Iron Man: No respawns
  • Gun Shortage: Limited ammo
  • Thrifty: No defense kits spawn
  • Pinned Down: Cannot move while shooting

Rewards: Bonus currency, exclusive cosmetics, achievement progress

How do I prepare for Extreme difficulty?

Requirements for success:

  • Prestige 4+ class with red perks
  • Tier 5 weapons minimum
  • Map knowledge - Know every spawn and defense spot
  • Coordinated team - Random teams rarely succeed
  • Voice communication - Essential for coordination

Key differences on Extreme:

  • 50% friendly fire damage
  • No player outlines through walls
  • Extremely limited resources
  • Multiple special spawns constantly
  • One mistake = team wipe
Start with: "Moscow 1" or "New York 1" - considered easiest Extreme maps.
Is there any post-launch content planned?

Regular updates include:

  • Seasonal events (Halloween, Christmas, etc.)
  • New challenge modes monthly
  • Balance patches and bug fixes
  • Occasional new weapons or variants

Recent major updates:

  • March 2024: Prestige levels 6-9 with new red perks
  • Console next-gen upgrades
  • New melee weapons added regularly
The game is still actively supported as of 2025 with no announced end date.
