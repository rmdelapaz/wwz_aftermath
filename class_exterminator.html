Exterminator ‚Äî Class Guide

Spoiler-light, beginner-focused build and habits.

Identity & role

Role

Crowd control via fire and area denial.

Why pick

Excellent at stabilizing swarms for struggling teams.

At a glance

flowchart TD A[Exterminator] --> B[Team Value] B --> C[Primary: Crowd control via fire and area denial] B --> D[Secondary: Survivability] B --> E[Utility]

Starter build (new player friendly)

Designed to be safe and simple. Exact perk names vary by patch; focus on the effect described.

  • Stack perks that improve molotov uptime and increase fire area/duration.
  • Carry SMG/Shotgun for close-range add clear.
  • Pick utilities that slow and funnel (barbed wire, voltage).

Pair with teammates who can cover your weaknesses (see Classes Hub for synergies).

Simple gameplay loop

flowchart LR Scan[Scan the lane] --> Prep[Pre-place utility] Prep --> Engage[Engage the horde] Engage --> Stabilize[Stabilize with class tools] Stabilize --> Recover[Ammo/Heal/Regroup] Recover --> Scan

Tips

  • Throw fire slightly up-lane so the horde walks into it.
  • Avoid blocking teammates‚Äô sight lines with smoke/fire at long range.
  • Synergize with Fixer ammo drops before major holds.
