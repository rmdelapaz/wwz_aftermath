üéØ Recommended Builds

üåü Beginner Build - "Fire Starter"

Focus on basic fire control and survival. Perfect for learning positioning.

  • Priority: Molotov duration and equipment charges
  • Key Focus: Pyromaniac, Broad Shoulders, Wheatgrass
  • Playstyle: Block paths with fire, control swarm flow
  • Weapons: Shotgun + SMG for close-medium range
üí° Tip: Throw molotovs at choke points before swarms arrive to create fire barriers!

üî• Inferno Build - "Scorched Earth"

Maximum fire damage and area coverage. Turn the map into hell.

  • Priority: Fire spread and persistent burning
  • Key Focus: Scorched Earth, All Fried Up, Fire Trap
  • Playstyle: Chain fire kills to cover entire areas
  • Weapons: Flamethrower when available!
üí° Tip: Fire trails from Scorched Earth stack - create overlapping burn zones!

üí£ Demolition Build - "Boom Expert"

Focus on explosives and claymores. Maximum instant damage.

  • Priority: Explosive damage and claymore enhancement
  • Key Focus: Heavy Metal, Welcoming Party, Directed Blast
  • Playstyle: Pre-place claymores, RPG for emergencies
  • Weapons: Grenade Launcher + Shotgun
üí° Tip: Claymores can be stacked for massive damage to Bulls!

üõ°Ô∏è Fortress Build - "Defense Master"

Specialized for defense sections and holdouts. Insane stationary damage.

  • Priority: Defense bonuses and kit effectiveness
  • Key Focus: Home Guard, Hangover, Bullet Rain
  • Playstyle: Set up kill zones during defense
  • Weapons: MG5 or other stationary weapons
üí° Tip: Home Guard gives +100% damage - you become a turret during swarms!

üßü Special Zombie Counter-Strategies

üêÇ Bull

  • Pre-place claymores in charge paths
  • Direct molotov hits stagger briefly
  • Fire patches slow charge speed
  • RPG to the face stops charge instantly

ü§¢ Gasbag

  • NEVER use fire on Gasbag - instant explosion!
  • Let teammates handle while you clear surroundings
  • If alone, use primary weapon only
  • Create fire barrier between Gasbag and team

üëª Lurker

  • Ghost Town perk makes you invisible to Lurkers
  • Molotov the ground where they hide
  • Claymores at common lurk spots
  • Fire reveals their position when burning

üò± Screamer

  • Molotov prevents scream if direct hit
  • Always carry silenced weapon as backup
  • If scream starts, pre-fire swarm spawns
  • Claymores at Screamer spawn points

üéØ Bomber

  • Fire instantly detonates them (use carefully)
  • Can create chain reactions in groups
  • Molotov behind them to push toward enemies
  • Never fire when near teammates

üìç Situational Tactics

üåä Swarm Defense

  1. Pre-fire all approach routes
  2. Layer claymores at choke points
  3. Save 1 molotov for emergency
  4. Focus pyramid bases with fire
  5. Use Home Guard position for +100% damage

üèÉ Running Sections

  1. Throw molotovs backward while running
  2. Fire at doorways to block chasers
  3. Quick claymore drops at turns
  4. Use Ghost Town for emergency escape
  5. Clear path ahead with shotgun

üëπ Boss Fights

  1. Save equipment for add phases
  2. Create fire perimeter around team
  3. Claymores at add spawn doors
  4. Direct fire damage on boss weak points
  5. Scorched Earth creates safe zones

üõ°Ô∏è Escort Missions

  1. Fire walls protect objective path
  2. Pre-place claymores at stop points
  3. Molotov ahead to clear path
  4. Save equipment for final push
  5. Create burn zones at spawn points

üìã Quick Reference

üî• Fire Zone Placement

  1. Doorways - Block entry points
  2. Stairs - Zombies bunch up
  3. Corners - Create turn traps
  4. Pyramid bases - Prevent climbing
  5. Escape routes - Cover retreats

üí£ Claymore Best Spots

  • Fence breaks: Guaranteed multi-kills
  • Door frames: Funnel points
  • Bull paths: Instant stops
  • Climb spots: Clear clusters
  • Swarm spawns: Thin the herd

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Exterminator Mistakes to Avoid

  • Wasting molotovs: Don't throw at small groups
  • Bad fire placement: Blocking team movement
  • Firing Gasbags: Instant team wipe!
  • Hoarding equipment: Use it or lose it
  • Ignoring choke points: Control the flow
  • Forgetting Home Guard: +100% damage is huge
  • Poor claymore timing: Place before combat

üåü Tips for New Exterminator Players

  1. Learn the maps - Know choke points and spawn locations
  2. Pre-place everything - Set traps before combat
  3. Fire lasts long - Don't overlap unnecessarily
  4. Manage equipment - Job Satisfaction refills charges
  5. Communicate fires - Don't trap teammates
  6. Master Ghost Town - 3 seconds of safety
  7. Stack effects - Fire + claymore + scorched earth
  8. Defense positioning - Find your +100% damage spot
