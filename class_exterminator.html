üî• Exterminator Skill Tree

How the Skill System Works:
  • Progress through levels 1-30 to unlock new perk segments
  • Choose ONE perk per segment to activate
  • Red perks are special alternatives that cost more (red coins)
  • You can buy multiple perks in each segment but only use one at a time
  • Perks cost 150-500 yellow coins, red perks cost 150-500 red coins
  • Level 10, 20, and 30 perks are automatic and always active

‚≠ê Prestige Rank Perks

About Prestige Perks:
  • Unlocked by reaching Prestige Ranks 1-9
  • These are PASSIVE bonuses that are always active once unlocked
  • Stack with your regular perks for even more power
  • Each costs 7500 yellow coins to unlock
  • Persist through prestige resets

Prestige Progression

Rank 1: Wheatgrass II
Health increased by 5%.
Stacks with regular Wheatgrass for 30% total health increase.
Rank 2: Bullet Rain II
Shotgun and semi-automatic weapons reload speed increased by 10%.
Perfect for Exterminator's shotgun-heavy playstyle.
Rank 3: Know-How II
Molotovs and Claymores can kill 10% more targets.
Stacks with Know-How for 35% more kills with equipment.
Rank 4: Deep Pockets II
Carried ammo capacity for primary weapons increased by 10%.
More ammo for sustained crowd control.
Rank 6: Dragon Hide II
Fire resistance for you and your team is increased by 10%.
Team-wide fire protection - great for fire-heavy strategies.
Rank 7: Roseship
Barbed wire can kill 10% more targets for the whole team.
Improves all defensive structures team-wide.
Rank 8: Oily Hands
Molotov throw distance is increased by 25%.
Throw fire from safer distances - crucial for higher difficulties.
Rank 9: Bandolier II
All defense kits you install can kill 10% more targets and have 10% more ammo capacity.
The ultimate defensive improvement - all your installations are enhanced.
üí° Prestige Strategy: Focus on reaching at least Prestige Rank 3 for Know-How II, which directly improves your core equipment. Oily Hands at Rank 8 is particularly valuable for safer molotov throws. Dragon Hide II at Rank 6 protects your whole team from your own fire!

üßü Special Zombie Counter-Strategies

üêÇ Bull

  • Stack multiple Claymores in charge paths
  • Fire Trap Claymores stop and burn simultaneously
  • Direct molotov can stagger briefly
  • Heavy Metal RPG instant kills

ü§¢ Gasbag

  • Dragon Hide makes you immune to gas explosion
  • NEVER throw molotov directly at Gasbag
  • Fire on ground is safe - just not direct hits
  • Let gun-focused classes handle

üëª Lurker

  • Molotov ground where they're hiding
  • Rooted perk prevents pins
  • Ghost perk makes you invisible to them
  • Fire reveals cloaked Lurkers

üò± Screamer

  • Impromptu perk - molotov stuns them
  • Direct hit prevents scream
  • Pre-fire swarm spawn points if scream starts
  • Always carry silenced backup weapon

üìç Situational Tactics

üåä Swarm Defense

  1. Pre-place Fire Trap Claymores at climb points
  2. Molotov approach routes before swarm arrives
  3. Use Firestarter + Burnout for 50% damage bonus
  4. Save one molotov for pyramid emergency
  5. Bandolier helps team's defenses last longer

üèÉ Running Sections

  1. Throw molotovs backward while running
  2. All Elbows prevents slowdown when swarmed
  3. Quick Claymore drops at corners
  4. Cloak and Dagger for emergency invisibility
  5. Dragon Hide lets you run through your own fire

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Exterminator Mistakes to Avoid

  • Wasting molotovs early: Save for swarms and emergencies
  • Bad fire placement: Don't block team escape routes
  • Igniting Gasbags: Instant explosion kills nearby teammates
  • Not using Dragon Hide: Essential for fire immunity
  • Forgetting Firestarter bonus: 25% damage during swarms!
  • Poor Claymore placement: Place before combat starts

üåü Tips for New Exterminator Players

  1. Learn choke points - Every map has perfect fire zones
  2. Pre-place equipment - Set up before swarms
  3. Dragon Hide is mandatory - Fire immunity saves lives
  4. Manage resources - Don't waste on small groups
  5. Stack fire effects - Molotov + Fire Trap + Burnout
  6. Communicate fire zones - Don't trap teammates
  7. Use height advantage - Throw molotovs from above
  8. Master Pocket Factory timing - 150s for full recharge
