Spoiler-light guide for new WWZ: Aftermath players. How FF works, what alters it, and simple habits to avoid team damage.

At a Glance

Core Rules

  • Friendly fire is always on in PvE and scales with difficulty
  • Melee is FF-safe - you won't hurt teammates with swings
  • Explosives & fire can down allies - treat heavies and grenades as "callout only"

Good Habits

  • When stacked shoulder-to-shoulder: call "melee only" until spacing improves

  • Ping specials before shooting past teammates

Communication & Safety Checklist

  2. Count blasts. "Frag out" / "RPG in 3‚Ä¶2‚Ä¶1"
  3. Ping specials before threading shots past teammates
  4. Use class cover: Drone on heavy-user, Medic Lv21, Vanguard shield for pushes
  5. Reset if tilted. A calm squad makes fewer FF mistakes

Advanced Tips

Weapon-Specific FF Notes

  • Shotguns: Wide spread = higher FF risk at close range
  • Crossbow: Explosive bolts have splash damage
  • Heavy Weapons: All have significant FF potential
  • Mortars: Team-wide danger during setup

Situational Awareness

  • Doorways: Never shoot through teammates in chokes
  • Pyramids: Wait for clear shot angles
  • Swarm Defense: Establish firing lanes early
  • Retreat: Cover fire from sides, not through team
