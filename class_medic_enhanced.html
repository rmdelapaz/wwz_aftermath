üßü Special Zombie Counter-Strategies (Medic-Specific)

üêÇ BULL

Medic Strategy:

  • Pre-heal teammates in Bull's path for damage reduction buff
  • Save Stim Pistol for instant recovery after charge
  • Position yourself perpendicular to team - never in line
  • If teammate is grabbed, stun grenade interrupts the slam

Equipment: Stun Grenades or Molotovs to stop charges

ü§¢ GASBAG

Medic Strategy:

  • Maintain maximum distance - gas cloud devastates teams
  • Pre-apply temp health before engaging
  • Have Stim ready for teammates caught in gas
  • Never melee - instant gas explosion

Equipment: Stay back and use ranged weapons only

üëª LURKER

Medic Strategy:

  • Listen for growls - audio cue before pounce
  • Travel in buddy system - immediate rescue possible
  • Masking gas makes you invisible during revives
  • Stim Pistol can headshot Lurkers with Lobotomy perk

Equipment: Flashbangs reveal hiding spots

üò± SCREAMER

Medic Strategy:

  • Priority target #1 - scream triggers swarm
  • Use Stim Pistol for silent ranged kills
  • Always carry silenced weapon as Medic
  • If scream starts, immediately prep AoE healing

Equipment: Silenced weapons mandatory

üìç Situational Tactics

Swarm Defense Priorities:

  1. Set up near ammo box but not blocking it
  2. Pre-heal team for damage resistance
  3. Save medkits for mid-swarm emergencies
  4. Use Stim on pyramid climbers
  5. Focus fire on special zombies first

Running Section Priorities:

  1. Stay in middle of group - not front or back
  2. Heal while moving with Stim Pistol
  3. Drop medkits for slower teammates
  4. Save equipment for checkpoint defense
  5. Call out special spawns immediately

Boss Fight Priorities:

  1. Designate tank player to heal priority
  2. Manage medkit economy for phases
  3. Use Stim for burst damage phases
  4. Position for quick revives
  5. Save masking gas for critical revives

Escort Mission Priorities:

  1. One player pushes, Medic supports
  2. Rotate temp health on pusher
  3. Clear path with equipment
  4. Stim Pistol for emergency saves
  5. Maintain defensive perimeter

üìã Quick Reference

üíä Medkit Priority Order

  1. Pinned player - Immediate danger
  2. Last stand player - 5 second window
  3. Low health DPS - Keeps damage output high
  4. Low health tank - Maintains frontline
  5. Self (if under 50%) - Can't heal if dead

  • Retreat: Boost runner's speed
  • Pyramid climber: Prevent wipe

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Medic Mistakes to Avoid

  • Overhealing: Wasting medkits on 90% health teammates
  • Hoarding Stim: Not using it proactively (it's on cooldown, use it!)
  • Bad positioning: Too far back to reach pinned teammates
  • Ignoring masking gas: Not using it for dangerous revives
  • Tunnel vision: Focusing only on healing, not killing zombies
  • Poor economy: Using medkits when natural healing would work

üåü Tips for New Medics

  1. Start with Normal difficulty - Learn healing timing without pressure
  2. Follow the tank - They know where danger comes from
  3. Communicate constantly - Call out your cooldowns
  4. Watch the kill feed - Tells you who needs help
  5. Learn audio cues - Each special has unique sounds
  6. Practice aim with Stim - It has travel time
  7. Don't chase kills - Your job is keeping everyone up
