Tuning tips for brand‚Äënew players

  • Sensitivity: Start low. Raise until you can track a running zombie across your screen smoothly.
  • Aim assist: If on controller, enable it early; you can lower it later.
  • Audio: Boost dialogue & SFX to hear specials over gunfire; use stereo headphones.
  • Key rebinding: On PC, keep R reload and E interact separate to avoid accidental cancels.
