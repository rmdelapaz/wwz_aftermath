üìã Quick Reference

üõ°Ô∏è Shield Management

  • Angle matters: Face main threat
  • Regeneration: Lower shield to recharge
  • Charge timing: Build between waves
  • Breaking point: Know when to retreat
  • Team positioning: Keep allies behind

‚ö° Electric Charge Best Uses

  • Pyramid climbers: One bash clears all
  • Bull charges: Instant stop
  • Dense crowds: Maximum chain damage
  • Special kills: Thunder Strike
  • Emergency escape: Clear path

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Vanguard Mistakes to Avoid

  • Wrong angle: Not facing main threat
  • Blocking team: Standing in firing lanes
  • Wasting charges: Using on small groups
  • No regeneration: Keeping broken shield up
  • Solo tanking: Getting surrounded alone
  • Ignoring flanks: Only watching front
  • Poor communication: Not telling team your position

üåü Tips for New Vanguard Players

  1. Learn angles - Shield has limited coverage
  2. Manage charges - Don't waste on singles
  3. Position for team - You're mobile cover
  4. Watch shield health - Know when to lower
  5. Master the bash - Timing is everything
  6. Communicate constantly - Team needs to know where you are
  7. Use pistols - You can shoot with shield up
  8. Learn spawn points - Pre-position for waves
