üåü Tips for New Fixer Players

  1. Place bags strategically: Before defense points, not randomly
  2. Masking saves lives: Use for revives and escapes
  3. Explosive ammo priority: Give to high DPS teammates first
  4. Please Stand Up is clutch: Can save entire runs at level 10
  5. Corporate Power Nap: Level 30 auto-reload is game-changing
  6. Revive bonuses stack: Multiple perks trigger on revive
  7. Side Effects kills: Masking gas damages zombies
  8. One for the Road trade-off: More bags but weaker effects
  9. DLC perks are powerful: Turret buffs and team damage
  10. Prestige priority: My Round III for team ammo capacity
