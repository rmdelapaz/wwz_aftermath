üíº Core Ability

Supply Bags

Effect: Drop ammo crates that instantly refill primary weapon ammo and grant special ammo types

Cooldown: 90 seconds (can be reduced with perks)

Base Bonus: Team carries 10% more ammo for primary and secondary weapons

Pro Tip: Drop bags BEFORE combat starts, not during! The explosive ammo is a massive damage boost.

üéØ Recommended Builds

üåü Beginner Build - "Resource Manager"

Focus on keeping the team supplied with basic resources. Perfect for learning support timing.

  • Priority: Supply management and equipment efficiency
  • Key Perks: Focus on ammo capacity, equipment charges, and bag duration
  • Playstyle: Drop bags before fights, save equipment for emergencies
  • Weapons: Assault Carbine + Compact SMG for versatility
üí° Tip: Always drop bags at defensive positions BEFORE combat starts!

üí• Explosive Support Build - "Boom Supplier"

Maximum damage boost for the team through explosive ammo. Essential for higher difficulties.

  • Priority: Explosive ammo duration and effectiveness
  • Key Focus: Explosive ammo perks, bag cooldown reduction
  • Playstyle: Coordinate explosive ammo timing with team's DPS phases
  • Weapons: Assault Shotgun + Grenade Launcher for crowd clear
üí° Tip: Call out when dropping explosive ammo so DPS classes can maximize damage!

üëª Stealth Support Build - "Ghost Protocol"

Focus on masking gas for objectives and emergency escapes. Great for escort missions.

  • Priority: Masking gas effectiveness and utility
  • Key Focus: Masking gas perks, equipment charges
  • Playstyle: Save masking for objectives, revives, and escapes
  • Weapons: Silenced weapons mandatory
üí° Tip: Masking gas makes the entire team invisible - perfect for objective interactions!

  2. Place all equipment at choke points
  3. Save one bag for mid-swarm emergency
  4. Use masking gas if position overrun
  5. Prioritize heavy weapon users for ammo

üëπ Boss Fights

üìã Quick Reference

üíº Supply Bag Priority Order

  1. Heavy weapon users - Maximum damage output
  2. Hellraiser/Exterminator - Explosive classes benefit most
  3. Gunslinger - High ammo consumption
  4. Support classes - Sustained fire needed
  5. Melee classes - Lower priority

‚è±Ô∏è Ability Timing Guide

  • Pre-swarm: Drop explosive ammo bag
  • Defense start: Place all equipment
  • Mid-fight: Second bag if needed
  • Emergency: Masking gas for escape
  • Post-fight: Resupply heavy weapons

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Fixer Mistakes to Avoid

  • Hoarding bags: Use them proactively, not reactively
  • Poor timing: Dropping bags during combat instead of before
  • Ignoring heavy weapons: They need your ammo most
  • Wasting masking gas: Save for objectives and emergencies
  • Solo play: Not coordinating explosive ammo timing
  • Bad positioning: Bags in unsafe or hard-to-reach spots

üåü Tips for New Fixer Players

  1. Learn spawn timings - Drop bags before fights, not during
  2. Communicate constantly - Tell team when explosive ammo is active
  3. Position bags safely - Near cover but accessible
  4. Watch ammo counts - Know who needs resupply
  5. Master masking gas - Practice timing for objectives
  6. Coordinate with Medic - You two are the support backbone
  7. Don't forget equipment - It's not just about ammo bags
  8. Please Stand Up perk - Can save entire runs when maxed
