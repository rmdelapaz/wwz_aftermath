üíº Core Ability: Mule

Supply Bag Mastery

Starting Equipment: Supply Bag and Scout Rifle

Team Buff: Increase carried ammo capacity for primary and secondary weapons by 10% for all teammates

Base Uses: 4 uses per bag (can be increased to 5 with perks)

üéì Advanced Fixer Techniques

üìç Supply Bag Positioning

  • Pre-fight: Always drop before combat, never during
  • Safe zones: Behind cover but easily accessible
  • Choke points: Where team naturally gathers to fight
  • Defense positions: At the main hold point, not scattered
  • Escape routes: Along retreat paths for running sections

‚è±Ô∏è Timing Mastery

  • Explosive Ammo Window: Lasts ~30 seconds base, up to 37.5 with Efficiency
  • Masking Duration: 6 seconds base, up to 8 with perks
  • Equipment Cooldowns: Track your 90-second windows
  • Please Stand Up: 60-second cooldown - save for true emergencies
  • Corporate Power Nap: Every 10 seconds - remind team to weapon swap

üéØ Priority System

  1. Heavy Weapon Users: They benefit most from explosive ammo
  2. Hellraiser/Exterminator: Explosive classes get double value
  3. Gunslinger: High ammo consumption needs constant supply
  4. Medic/Dronemaster: Support classes need sustained fire
  5. Slasher/Vanguard: Lower priority but still important

üßü Special Zombie Counter-Strategies

üêÇ Bull

  • Drop explosive ammo bag before Bull spawns for instant kill potential
  • Breaching Charge in charge path for guaranteed stun
  • Masking gas makes entire team invisible to charge targeting
  • Stand by Me gives 100% damage boost if teammate gets pinned

ü§¢ Gasbag

  • Explosive ammo makes Gasbag pop much faster and safer
  • Side Effects masking gas can damage Gasbag while team is invisible
  • Maintain maximum range when team engages
  • Supply bags after kill to refill spent ammo

üëª Lurker

  • Masking grenades prevent Lurker targeting entirely
  • Cloakroom gives 3-second invisibility during pounce saves
  • Explosive ammo one-shots on headshot
  • Paramedic + Paramedic II = 125% faster pounce saves

üßä Bomber

  • Breaching Charges can detonate Bombers safely from range
  • Masking gas prevents Bomber aggro during objectives
  • Supply bags crucial after Bomber swarm triggers
  • Side Effects can kill Bombers through masking gas

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Fixer Mistakes to Avoid

  • Reactive bag dropping: Waiting until mid-fight to drop bags
  • Poor positioning: Bags in exposed or hard-to-reach locations
  • Hoarding equipment: Saving everything "for later" that never comes
  • Ignoring heavy weapons: Not prioritizing payload/MGL users
  • Wasting masking: Using for non-critical situations
  • No communication: Not calling out explosive ammo timing
  • Forgetting Corporate Power Nap: Team doesn't know about auto-reload
  • Bad Please Stand Up timing: Using it too early in fights

üåü Tips for New Fixer Players

  1. Learn spawn triggers: Know when swarms/specials appear to pre-drop bags
  2. Watch the killfeed: Track who's getting kills to know who needs ammo
  3. Communicate constantly: "Explosive ammo active!" is crucial info
  4. Master the 90-second cycle: Your equipment cooldown rhythm
  5. Position yourself centrally: You need to reach everyone quickly
  6. Save one bag minimum: Always keep emergency supplies available
  7. Practice masking timing: 6-8 seconds goes fast - make it count
  8. Coordinate with Medic: You two are the team's foundation
  9. Track special zombies: Scavenger procs every 90 seconds on special kills
  10. Prestige early: Those permanent perks are worth the temporary loss
