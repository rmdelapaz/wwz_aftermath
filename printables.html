Controls quick card

Xbox & PlayStation

  • Fire: RT / R2 · Aim: LT / L2
  • Reload/Interact: X / □ · Swap: Y / △
  • Melee: RS / R3 · Equipment: RB / R1
  • Class Skill: LB / L1 · Sprint: LS / L3
  • Crouch: B / ◯ · Jump: A / ✕

PC — KB/M

  • Fire: Mouse 1 · Aim: Mouse 2
  • Reload: R · Interact: E · Swap: 1/2 or Wheel
  • Melee: V · Equipment: Q · Skill: F
  • Sprint: Shift · Crouch: Ctrl · Ping: MMB/Z
