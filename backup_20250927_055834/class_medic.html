Identity & role

Role

Healer/support. Keeps people upright, accelerates revives, and reduces damage taken.

Why pick

Safest pick for learning; you always bring value and can bail out mistakes.

At a glance

Starter build (new player friendly)

Designed to be safe and simple. Exact perk names vary by patch; focus on the effect described.

  • Prioritize perks that improve medkit efficiency, speed up revives, or grant temporary DR (damage reduction) after healing.
  • Take an SMG or carbine with controllable recoil; you don’t need max DPS.
  • Carry a stun or smoke utility to create revive windows during swarms.

Pair with teammates who can cover your weaknesses (see Classes Hub for synergies).

Simple gameplay loop

flowchart LR Scan[Scan the lane] --> Prep[Pre-place utility] Prep --> Engage[Engage the horde] Engage --> Stabilize[Stabilize with class tools] Stabilize --> Recover[Ammo/Heal/Regroup] Recover --> Scan

Tips

  • Stay slightly behind the frontliner so you can reach downed teammates safely.
  • Save one heal for after a major swarm to top off the team before moving.
  • Ping medkits/ammo for others; you set the team’s sustain rhythm.
