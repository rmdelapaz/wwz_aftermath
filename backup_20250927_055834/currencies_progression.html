What each currency does

Supplies (yellow)

  • Common currency used for most unlocks: weapon upgrades and many perks.
  • Earned from missions and Horde Mode; used for buying defenses in Horde.

See: community wiki entries on Supplies and Weapons.

Challenge Coins (blue)

  • Rarer currency for unique weapons/cosmetics and certain unlocks.
  • Primarily from Daily/Weekly Challenges and higher-difficulty clears.

See: community wiki entry on Challenge Coins.

Beginner-friendly earnings

  • Supplies: Standard campaign missions on Normal, or early Horde waves, reliably award yellow coins while you learn.
  • Challenge Coins: Complete Daily/Weekly Challenges on Normal/Hard with friends; pick up Virus Samples for bonus blue coins on mission completion.

Class XP vs Weapon Progress

  • Class XP: Unlocks perks along a class tree and prestige tiers.
  • Weapon levels: Unlock stronger variants and perk slots; some upgrades require you to purchase the next level before XP applies.

Simple farming flow

flowchart LR A[Daily/Weekly Challenge] --> B{Win?} B -- Yes --> C[Blue Coins + Supplies] B -- No --> D[Supplies only / retry] C --> E[Spend on key class perks] E --> F[Upgrade 1-2 core weapons]

Tip: Avoid spreading coins across too many weapons early. Focus on one primary and a sidearm you like.
