Identity & role

Role

Ammo & utility. Supply bags and defensive gadgets that smooth runs.

Why pick

Makes every team more consistent—great for new squads learning maps.

At a glance

Starter build (new player friendly)

Designed to be safe and simple. Exact perk names vary by patch; focus on the effect described.

  • Pick perks that increase ammo supplied and restore equipment to the team.
  • Favor a battle rifle/AR that handles mid-range with low recoil.
  • Bring defensive gadgets that slow hordes (barbed wire/voltage).

Pair with teammates who can cover your weaknesses (see Classes Hub for synergies).

Simple gameplay loop

flowchart LR Scan[Scan the lane] --> Prep[Pre-place utility] Prep --> Engage[Engage the horde] Engage --> Stabilize[Stabilize with class tools] Stabilize --> Recover[Ammo/Heal/Regroup] Recover --> Scan

Tips

  • Drop ammo before a big hold, not during—announce it with a ping.
  • Place defenses to shape the horde path, not to finish them.
  • Coordinate with Exterminator for fire + chokepoint combos.
