üåü Tips for New Hellraiser Players

  1. Start with MGL: Only class with heavy weapon from start!
  2. Unshakeable is mandatory: 100% self-damage immunity
  3. C4 placement: Throw far ahead, detonate when surrounded
  4. Here Kitty strategy: Plant C4 as zombie lure
  5. Directed Blast doubles damage: 100% more explosive damage
  6. Demolition Squad: Level 10 buffs entire team's explosives
  7. Hollywood protects team: 30% less explosive damage to all
  8. Technician is huge: +25% kills on all explosives at level 30
  9. DLC perks synergy: Alert Red + Scorched Earth = area denial
  10. Prestige priority: Directed Blast II & III for kill potential
