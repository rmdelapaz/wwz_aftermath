üéØ Recommended Builds

üåü Beginner Build - "Boom Basics"

Focus on C4 management and self-preservation.

  • Priority: Extra charges and damage reduction
  • Key Focus: Welcome Mat, Deep Pockets, Effective Radius
  • Playstyle: Pre-place C4, detonate safely
  • Weapons: Shotgun + any backup
üí° Tip: Place C4 at choke points before swarms arrive for maximum effect!

üí£ Demolition Build - "Delete Button"

Maximum C4 damage and area coverage. Erase entire swarms.

  • Priority: Explosion size and damage
  • Key Focus: Directed Blast, Knock Knock, Hole Punch
  • Playstyle: Strategic placement for multi-kills
  • Weapons: Explosive Shotgun synergy
üí° Tip: Directed Blast + Hole Punch can clear entire corridors!

üîÑ Regeneration Build - "Endless Explosions"

  • Priority: C4 regeneration and charges
  • Key Focus: Free Refill, Job Satisfaction, Scavenger
  • Playstyle: Aggressive C4 usage, quick refills
  • Weapons: Fast-killing weapons for refills
üí° Tip: With proper kill chains, you can throw C4 every 15 kills!

üöÄ Heavy Artillery Build - "Walking Tank"

  • Playstyle: RPG/MGL primary, C4 backup

üìã Quick Reference

  1. Doorways: Maximum funnel damage
  2. Climb spots: Clear pyramid instantly
  3. Corners: Surprise detonation
  4. Spawn points: Pre-emptive clear
  5. Escape routes: Cover retreat

üí• Detonation Timing

  • Maximum density: Wait for groups
  • Special priority: Bulls and Gasbags first
  • Chain reaction: Use Bombers
  • Pyramid base: Single C4 clears all
  • Emergency only: Keep one reserved

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Hellraiser Mistakes to Avoid

  • Self-damage: Too close to own explosions
  • Team damage: C4 near teammates
  • Waste on small groups: Save for swarms
  • Poor timing: Detonating too early/late
  • Hoarding C4: Use them to get refills
  • Ignoring heavy weapons: They're powerful!
  • Bad placement: Missing choke points

üåü Tips for New Hellraiser Players

  1. Learn spawn points - Pre-place for maximum effect
  2. Master detonation timing - Wait for density
  3. Manage self-damage - Distance is key
  4. Chain your kills - Refills come from streaks
  5. Communicate C4 placement - Warn teammates
  6. Use Here Kitty wisely - 5-second timer
  7. Don't forget shotgun - Great for refills
  8. Practice with Directed Blast - Aim matters!
