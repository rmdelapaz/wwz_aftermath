Identity & role

Role

Explosives-focused DPS for clustered swarms.

Why pick

Deletes big waves when placed well; satisfying and powerful.

Starter build (new player friendly)

Designed to be safe and simple. Exact perk names vary by patch; focus on the effect described.

  • Invest in perks that increase explosive capacity/damage and reduce self-damage.
  • Carry a carbine or DMR to pick specials between explosives.
  • Practice grenade bank shots off walls into choke points.

Pair with teammates who can cover your weaknesses (see Classes Hub for synergies).

Simple gameplay loop

flowchart LR Scan[Scan the lane] --> Prep[Pre-place utility] Prep --> Engage[Engage the horde] Engage --> Stabilize[Stabilize with class tools] Stabilize --> Recover[Ammo/Heal/Regroup] Recover --> Scan

Tips

  • Call out throws so Slasher/Vanguard don’t push into your blast.
  • Avoid blowing up oil/gas at teammates’ feet—step forward first.
  • Use explosives to save a failed hold, not to pad damage.
