üìã Quick Reference

ü§ñ Drone Deployment Tips

  • Pre-combat: Deploy before engagement
  • High ground: Better drone coverage
  • Central position: Maximum team buff range
  • Safe timing: Not while being chased
  • Fresh deploy: Before major events

üéØ Priority Management

  • Let drone handle: Specials and climbers
  • You focus on: Objectives and team support
  • Combined fire: Bulls and bosses
  • Drone saves: Pinned teammates
  • Manual control: Mark priority targets

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Dronemaster Mistakes to Avoid

  • Late deployment: Deploy before combat, not during
  • Ignoring drone: It needs protection too
  • Poor positioning: Drone needs line of sight
  • Wasting on small fights: Save for swarms
  • Forgetting buffs: Stay near team for buffs
  • No communication: Tell team about drone status
  • Overlap with Vanguard: Coordinate deployments

üåü Tips for New Dronemaster Players

  2. Trust the drone - It's smarter than you think
  3. Position matters - High ground = better coverage
  4. Watch the timer - Know when it expires
  5. Protect your drone - It can be destroyed
  6. Coordinate with team - Buffs need proximity
  7. Learn spawn timings - Deploy for maximum value
  8. Master Buddy System - Two drones is game-changing
