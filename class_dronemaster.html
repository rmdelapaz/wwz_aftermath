ü§ñ Dronemaster Skill Tree

How the Skill System Works:
  • Progress through levels 1-30 to unlock new perk segments
  • Choose ONE perk per segment to activate
  • Red perks are special alternatives that cost more (red coins)
  • You can buy multiple perks in each segment but only use one at a time
  • Perks cost 150-500 yellow coins, red perks cost 150-500 red coins
  • Level 10, 20, and 30 perks are automatic and always active

‚≠ê Prestige Rank Perks

About Prestige Perks:
  • Unlocked by reaching Prestige Ranks 1-9
  • These are PASSIVE bonuses that are always active once unlocked
  • Stack with your regular perks for even more power
  • Each costs 7500 yellow coins to unlock
  • Persist through prestige resets

Prestige Progression

Rank 1: Ammo Drop
Person being followed regains 10% of primary ammo when Quadrocopter is depleted.
Free ammo restoration when drone expires - great for sustained fights.
Rank 2: Unfettered
Movement speed penalty when zombies are very close reduced by 50% for person followed by your Quadrocopter.
Helps teammates escape when swarmed.
Rank 3: Spotter
Firearm damage increased by 15% for person followed by your Quadrocopter.
Significant damage boost for whoever the drone follows.
Rank 4: Guardian Angel
Person followed by your Quadrocopter will take 10% less damage.
Passive damage reduction - stacks with other defensive perks.
Rank 6: Go The Distance II
Quadrocopter charge (the number of stun shots or kills the Quadrocopter can perform) is increased by 5%.
Stacks with regular Go The Distance for 20% more charge.
Rank 7: Handyman
Your Quadrocopter will automatically reload autoturrets within 10 meters (cooldown: 30 seconds).
Keeps defense turrets operational during swarms.
Rank 8: As a Team!
Players followed by your Drones set up defense kits 50% faster.
Speeds up critical defense preparations.
Rank 9: He Can Do It All!
Receive 2 seconds of invulnerability when the Quadrocopter is depleted (applies to the person it's following).
Brief invincibility window when drone expires - perfect for escapes.
üí° Prestige Strategy: Focus on reaching at least Prestige Rank 3 for Spotter's 15% damage boost - it's a massive team buff. Guardian Angel at Rank 4 adds survivability. The later ranks offer excellent utility but are less essential.

üìã Key Perk Details

Must-Have Perks

Sharing is Caring (Level 17-19)
Holding "Use Equipment" button commands Quadrocopter to follow a teammate you're aiming at. You must have a spare Quadrocopter and you can't have more than two Quadrocopters active at the same time.
Give drones to teammates for multiple active drones - game changer!
Pocket Factory (Level 24-26)
You won't be able to pick equipment bags on the mission but your equipment will be constantly regenerating (150 seconds to replenish to maximum).
Infinite drones over time - perfect for long missions.
Eye In The Sky (Level 21-23)
All active special zombies within a radius 14 meters around your Quadrocopter will be automatically marked.
Turns your drone into a mobile radar for the whole team.
Silent Stinger/Firecracker (Level 4-6)
Changes drone to pistol mode. Silent for stealth, or loud for double damage.
Pistol drones can kill 15 zombies vs 12 stuns - more efficient!
Clear! (Level 14-16 - Red)
If a player followed by a Quadrocopter is incapacitated, the Quadrocopter will revive them with a temporary health boost and get depleted (cooldown: 180 seconds).
Automatic revival - can save entire runs when teammates fall!

üìç Situational Tactics

üåä Swarm Defense

  1. Deploy drone before swarm starts
  2. Use Well Trained stationary mode for +50% damage
  3. Overdrive during pyramid climbs
  4. Share drones with defenders
  5. Rechargeable keeps drone active between waves

üèÉ Running Sections

  1. Deploy before running for coverage
  2. Pied Piper to distract chasers
  3. Drone follows and protects automatically
  4. Coming in Hot clears path if needed
  5. Parting Gift gives temp health for sprint

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Dronemaster Mistakes to Avoid

  • Late deployment: Deploy before combat starts
  • Wasting on specials: Stun costs 5 shots for specials!
  • Ignoring Rechargeable: Let drone idle to recharge
  • Not sharing: Give drones to teammates with Sharing is Caring
  • Poor positioning: Drone needs line of sight
  • Forgetting modes: Pistol often better than stun

üåü Tips for New Dronemaster Players

  1. Deploy early - Before combat for full effectiveness
  2. Learn the modes - Stun vs Pistol vs Stim
  3. Watch the charge - Specials cost 5 stun shots
  4. Use Rechargeable - 1% per second after 5s idle
  5. Share drones - Multiple active drones is powerful
  6. Position matters - Drone needs clear shots
  7. Save for swarms - Don't waste on small fights
  8. Master controls - Tap vs Hold for different functions
