Class snapshots

Medic

Role: Healer/support. Keep the team alive and moving.

Why pick: Easiest safety net for new squads.

Fixer

Role: Ammo & utility. Supply bags, team sustain.

Why pick: Smooths ammo economy; great for learning.

Role: Straightforward ranged DPS.

Why pick: Excellent horde control for beginners.
