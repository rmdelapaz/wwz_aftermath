Horde & Endgame

Wave rhythm

  • Place choke defenses first (barbed wire/voltage), then coverage (turrets/auto-turrets).
  • Assign lanes: left, right, mid, and a floater for specials.

Common wipe causes & fixes

  • No lane ownership: Assign lanes and rotate only on callouts.
