Universal mission habits

  • Noise discipline: Silence first wave with suppressed shots; alarms wake the neighborhood.
  • Loot phase: Clear room → then loot. Don’t split.
  • Hold prep: Barbed wire at chokepoints, voltage on breach lanes, turret covering flanks.
  • Specials callouts: Use ping + a single word: “Bull left!” “Gas mid!”

Stealth openers

  • Two players focus left/right sleepers; two watch for patrollers.
  • Avoid chain alarms; if one triggers, commit to clearing then re-enter stealth later.

Exact routes vary by episode; this page teaches transferable habits without revealing story beats.
