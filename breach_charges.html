üìä Understanding Breach Charges

What are Breach Charges?

Breach charges are powerful explosive equipment items that can:

  • Instant Kill: Eliminate large groups of zombies in one blast
  • Clear Paths: Open blocked routes and create escape paths
  • Defense Tool: Perfect for holding choke points during swarms
  • Special Zombie Counter: Instantly kill Bulls, Infectors, and other specials
  • Pyramid Buster: Destroy zombie pyramids climbing walls
Spawn Types: Guaranteed - Always spawns in this location Random - May or may not appear Conditional - Depends on difficulty or player count

üéØ Breach Charge Strategy Guide

Optimal Usage Tips

  • Save for Pyramids: Priority #1 - destroying climbing pyramids prevents breaches
  • Choke Points: Place at narrow passages during defense sections
  • Emergency Escape: Keep one for clearing escape routes when overwhelmed
  • Bull Counter: Instant kill on charging Bulls - saves the team
  • Remote Detonation: Can be triggered from distance for tactical advantage
  • Stack with Defenses: Combine with electric grids for maximum effect

Class Synergies

  • Fixer: Can carry extra charges with Equipment Bags perk
  • Hellraiser: Explosive damage perks increase breach charge effectiveness
  • Exterminator: Combine with Molotovs for area denial
  • Vanguard: Use charges to protect shield charge paths

üí° Pro Tips for Breach Charge Hunting

  • Difficulty Scaling: More breach charges spawn on higher difficulties (Insane/Extreme)
  • Player Count: 4-player games often have additional spawn points
  • Random Spawns: Some locations have 30-50% spawn chance - check every run
  • Look Up: Some charges spawn on upper floors or elevated platforms
  • Side Rooms: Always check optional rooms and dead-end corridors
  • Equipment Synergy: Breach charges often spawn near other equipment
  • Communication: Call out breach charge locations to teammates
  • Priority Targets: Pyramids > Bulls > Dense Swarms > Regular zombies

üõ°Ô∏è Optimal Breach Charge Placement

Top Defense Locations by Mission Type:

Pyramid Climb Points:

  • Place directly on wall where pyramid forms
  • Wait until pyramid is 50-75% formed for maximum kills
  • One charge can eliminate an entire pyramid

Tunnel/Corridor Defense:

  • Place at narrowest point of passage
  • Combine with auto-turret for overlapping coverage
  • Remote detonate when swarm is bunched up

Open Area Defense:

  • Place at entry points rather than center
  • Use to cut off flanking routes
  • Save one for emergency extraction path

Escort Missions:

  • Place ahead on the escort path
  • Clear blocked doorways proactively
  • Keep one mobile for Bull encounters

‚ùå Common Breach Charge Mistakes to Avoid

  • Wasting on Small Groups: Don't use on less than 20 zombies
  • Early Placement: Don't place all charges at start of defense
  • Forgetting to Detonate: Remember to trigger before retreating
  • Overlapping Charges: Space them out to cover more area
  • Hoarding: Use them! They don't carry over between sections
  • Friendly Fire: Watch teammate positions before detonating
  • Ignoring Audio Cues: Listen for pyramid formation sounds
