Glossary (spoiler-light)

Supplies (yellow)

General currency for upgrades/perks and Horde purchases.

Challenge Coins (blue)

Reward from Daily/Weekly challenges and harder clears; used for select unlocks/cosmetics.

Virus Samples

Carryable bonus items that increase end-of-mission rewards.

Horde Mode XL

Endless wave mode. Spend smart, assign lanes, and plan emergency buys.

Specials

Unique infected with abilities. Common calls: Bull, Gasbag, Lurker, Screamer.

Choke

Defensible position where you funnel hordes through slows and fire.
